Bottom Line

The verdict

The revision shows clear progress -adding gold, international, and multi-asset exposure addresses real gaps. However, some trade-offs were introduced: the strongest core fund was removed, high-correlation funds were retained, and complexity increased. Overall, the revision is a lateral move -meaningful improvements in some areas, offset by new structural challenges.
Priority areas for this profile:
1. A dedicated debt allocation (20%+) -standard for this age and risk profile
2. A diversified core equity fund as the portfolio anchor
3. Developed market international exposure (e.g. S&P 500) for lower correlation with India
4. Fewer funds with lower inter-fund correlation -efficiency over breadth
5. A Balanced Advantage Fund for automatic equity/debt rebalancing
Highest-impact change: Adding a dedicated short-duration debt fund at 20-25% of the portfolio would significantly improve downside protection and provide a stable base for rebalancing during market corrections.
