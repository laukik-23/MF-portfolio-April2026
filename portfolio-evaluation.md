## G. Key Recommendations

### 1. Reduce Fund Count from 9 to 5-6
Too many overlapping equity funds. Consider keeping:
- **HDFC Flexi Cap** — diversified core
- **Invesco Mid Cap** — mid-cap exposure
- **Bandhan Small Cap** — small-cap exposure
- Cut redundant funds that are highly correlated (e.g., SBI Contra duplicates HDFC Flexi Cap at 0.95 correlation)

### 2. Add Debt / Fixed Income Allocation
At least **15-20%** in a short-duration or dynamic bond fund for downside protection.

### 3. Add Gold Exposure
**5-10%** via a gold fund/ETF. Sundaram Multi Asset includes some gold (2.3%), but it's too small at 10% of the portfolio.

### 4. Remove or Reduce DSP Banking & Financial Svcs
Portfolio already has **28% financial services** exposure from other funds. This fund is doubling down on sector concentration.

### 5. Consider International Diversification
**10-15%** in a US/global equity fund to reduce single-country risk.

### 6. Implement Rebalancing
The Hypothetical report used no rebalancing. Annual or semi-annual rebalancing would improve risk-adjusted returns.

### 7. Suitability

**This portfolio IS suitable for:**
- An aggressive investor with **10+ year horizon**
- High risk tolerance — can stomach **30%+ drawdowns**

**This portfolio is NOT suitable for:**
- Conservative or moderate investors
- Anyone needing money within 5 years
- Anyone uncomfortable with concentrated equity exposure
